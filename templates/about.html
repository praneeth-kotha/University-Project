🌱 About Smart Farm AI

Empowering farmers with Artificial Intelligence to make smarter, data-driven agricultural decisions.

Smart Farm AI is an intelligent crop recommendation system that analyzes soil nutrients, environmental conditions, and climate data to suggest the most suitable crops.

🌾 Soil Analysis

Analyzes Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium levels.

🌦 Climate Monitoring

Evaluates temperature, humidity, and rainfall.

🤖 AI Prediction

Uses machine learning to recommend best crops.

🚜 Benefits for Farmers

  • ✔ Increased crop yield
  • ✔ Reduced farming risks
  • ✔ Better planning & profit
  • ✔ Scientific decision-making

🌍 Our Vision

To bridge the gap between traditional farming and modern technology, enabling sustainable and intelligent agriculture worldwide.
